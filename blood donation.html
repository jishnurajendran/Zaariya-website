Blood Donation

Concern

Every year our nation requires about 4 crore units of blood out of which only a meagre 40 lakh units of blood are available. Almost every two seconds, someone needs blood. Unfortunately blood can’t be manufactured. It can only come from generous donors. Many healthy and educated individuals however don’t come forward to volunteer for this noble cause because of several misconceptions regarding blood donation.

Project Outline

Organisation of a blood donation campaign in the institute premises to create awareness among the students, faculties as well as staff and other campus residents about blood donation; provisions for an opportunity for voluntary donors to come forward and join us in the noble cause by donating blood.

Project accomplishment

The blood donation camp was successfully organised in NISER premises on 20.04.2015 in association with Sahaya, a Bhubaneswar based voluntary organisation which works to provide help to accident victims and create health awareness among people. A whooping 63 units of blood were donated to Capital Hospital Blood Bank.
