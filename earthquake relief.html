Earthquake relief

Concern

More than 6000 individuals lost their lives in the recent earthquakes that devastated our neighbouring country Nepal. Many more lost their family, their shelter and their sources of income. In this hour of need Zaariya volunteers decided to take a step to help the earthquake victims financially.

Project Outline

Organisation of a fund-raiser for collection of money; Identification of a suitable channel/organisation to reach out to the earthquake victims.

Project Accomplishments

An amount of Rs.14,590/- was collected in our first round of fund-raising which was handed over to two volunteers from Homenet Nepal and Play for Peace. The volunteers departed for Nepal on 20.05.2015 . Together they would go to the villages near Kathmandu and do trauma consultations. Affected women and children were provided basic necessities water, food, clothes, tents and other sanitary products.
